# DouXia

[中文介绍](README.zh.md)

![DouXia Logo](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/KylinLabAI/DouXia-App/master/resources/logo_256.png)

The desktop app that turns your scattered AI chats into organized, searchable, exportable knowledge.

## Features

- **Embedded AI Chat + Auto-Capture** — Use Doubao, Yuanbao, DeepSeek, or ChatGPT inside DouXia; every conversation is captured and stored locally in real time
- **Unified Conversation Management** — Browse, search (FTS5), and filter all AI conversations in one place regardless of source platform
- **Smart Organization** — AI auto-tagging, topic clustering, knowledge graph visualization, and custom tag management
- **AI-Powered Knowledge Extraction** — Concise summaries, structured knowledge cards, and AI-recommended cleanup of low-value conversations
- **Flexible Export & Sharing** — One-click export to Markdown, HTML, or PDF, plus LAN sharing and database backup/restore
- **Works Offline** — All data lives on your device; search, browse, and export without internet

## Quick Start

1. Download the installer for your platform
2. Run the installer and follow the prompts
3. Launch DouXia
4. Choose your preferred AI platform and log in within the embedded browser
5. Start chatting — conversations are auto-captured by default
