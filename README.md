# DouXia — Own Your AI Conversations

**The desktop app that turns your scattered AI chats into organized, searchable, exportable knowledge.**

[Download](#download)

---

## What is DouXia?

DouXia (豆匣) is a local-first desktop app that embeds your AI platforms — Doubao, Yuanbao, DeepSeek, ChatGPT — inside a native window. It auto-captures every conversation you have, stores them in a local SQLite database, and gives you powerful tools to search, organize, export, and clean up your chat history.

**Your conversations. Your computer. Your rules.**

## Supported Platforms

| Platform | Capture | Sync Delete | Status |
|----------|---------|-------------|--------|
| Doubao (豆包) | ✅ | ✅ Permanent | Available |
| Yuanbao (元宝) | ✅ | ✅ Permanent | Available |
| DeepSeek | ✅ | ✅ Permanent | Available |
| ChatGPT | ✅ | ⚠️ Archive only | Available |

## Key Features

- **Auto-capture** — Conversations saved in real time, no manual steps
- **Full-text search (FTS5)** — Find anything across all platforms instantly
- **Smart organization** — AI auto-tagging, topic clustering, knowledge graph
- **Knowledge extraction** — AI summaries and structured knowledge cards
- **Flexible export** — Markdown, HTML, PDF; single or batch
- **LAN sharing** — Browse your knowledge base from any device on your network
- **Batch cleanup** — Delete, archive, and organize hundreds of chats in seconds
- **Chinese & English** — Full bilingual interface

## Privacy

- **100% local** — All data stored in SQLite on your computer. Nothing uploaded.
- **No account required** — No sign-up, no cloud sync, no tracking.
- **Your API key** — Smart features use your own AI provider key.
